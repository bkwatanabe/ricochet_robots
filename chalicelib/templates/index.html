Rules

Ricochet Robots is a game where you navigate robots to destinations of the same color.
Robots move horizontally or vertically until they hit a wall or another robot.
The current destination is shown in the center of the board.
The rainbow destination is considered reached when the player moves any color robot to it.
All robots can be moved to make navigating to the destination possible.

Controls

Left Click/Tap - Select Robot
Arrow Buttons/Swipe - Move Selected Robot
